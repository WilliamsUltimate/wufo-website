La WUFA

Beautiful Animals, Destroyers of Worlds, We’re Worth A Lot!

Frisbee? Fun? Friends? We’ve got it all! La WUFA is Williams College’s ultimate frisbee team for female and non-binary students. In the fall, we welcome new team members, learn the beautiful game of Ultimate, and casually practice for our Spring season competing nationally at the USAU (USA Ultimate) DIII level. Interested in joining WUFA? No experience necessary! Fill out our interest form here, or email prb4@williams.edu if you want to get in contact. Hope to see you soon out on the sunny, grassy fields of Poker Flats (or an equally exciting Zoom “field”)!
