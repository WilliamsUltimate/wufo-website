La WUFA

Beautiful Animals, Destroyers of Worlds, We’re Worth A Lot!

La WUFA is Williams College’s ultimate frisbee team for female and trans students. We've been a student organization at Williams since 1986, and currently compete at the USAU DIII level. Recruiting takes place in the fall and we encourage those of any skill level to come try out — the vast majority of La WUFA members had no ultimate experience before their first practice!
